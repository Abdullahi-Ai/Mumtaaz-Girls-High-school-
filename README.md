# Mumtaaz Girls High School
## "Where Knowledge Meets Faith"

Mumtaaz Girls High School is a premier Islamic girls' institution located in Nakuru, Kenya. We are dedicated to nurturing disciplined, confident, and knowledgeable young Muslim women in a secure, faith-based environment that balances academic excellence with deep spiritual growth.

---

## 🎓 What We Offer

### 1. Academic Excellence (KCSE)
We provide a robust implementation of the Kenyan National Curriculum, focusing on preparing our students for top-tier university placement and professional success.
*   **Junior School (Grade 7)**: Bridging the gap between primary and secondary with a strong focus on core competencies.
*   **Senior School (Forms 3 & 4)**: Intensive KCSE preparation and career mentorship.
*   **Specialized Tracks**: STEM (Science, Tech, Engineering, Math), Humanities, and Technical Excellence.

### 2. Spiritual & Islamic Foundation
Our unique curriculum integrates spiritual depth into the daily academic life of our students.
*   **Quran Hifdh**: A comprehensive memorization program with specialized tracks for proficiency.
*   **Islamic Studies Certificate**: Specialized training in Fiqh (Jurisprudence), Seerah (Prophetic Biography), and Akhlaq (Character).
*   **Tarbiyah Focused**: A structured daily routine centered on character building and guided instruction.

### 3. Holistic Development
We believe in educating the whole person—heart, mind, and body.
*   **Creative Arts**: Drama, Music, and Theatre.
*   **Athletics**: Football, Netball, and Volleyball programs.
*   **Leadership Programs**: Mentorship and leadership training for future global citizens.
